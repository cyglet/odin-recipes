Mango smoothie

Description

It's an exotic drink that will give you energy.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium mango
  • 1 banana
  • 500ml orange juice
  • 4 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Cut the mango down either side of the flat stone, then peel and cut the flesh into chunks
  2. Peel and chop the banana
  3. Put all ingredients into a food processor or blender, then process until smooth and thick. Keep in the fridge and use the day you make it. Enjoy!
