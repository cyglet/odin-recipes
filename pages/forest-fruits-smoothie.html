Forest Fruits Shake

Description

Its a healthy drink you can take to school or work. It contains a lot of vitamins.

Ingredients

  • 100ml full-fat milk
  • 2 tbsp natural yogurt
  • 1 banana
  • 150g fruits of the forest
  • 50g blueberries
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/2 tbsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp goji berries
  • 1 tsp mixed seeds
  • 1 tsp honey

Steps

  1. Peel off a banana, rinse fruits and cut them into small pieces.
  2. Add fruits, milk, yogurt and goji berries into blender and blitz it.
  3. Add rest of the ingredients, pour into a glass or bottle and enjoy!
